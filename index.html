Top best place to visit in india

Uttarkashi is located 184 km away from Dehradun.
You can reach Uttarkashi from Dehradun by taking a train to Hardiwar first and then boarding a bus that does to Uttarkashi.
There are no direct trains from Dehradun to Uttarkashi.
The total journey will take you about 5 hours 40 minutes.
If you want to go by road, the best way is to take a State Transport Bus directly from Dehradun to Uttarkashi.
This journey will take you to your destination in about 4 hours 40 minutes.
This is also a cheaper way to reach Uttarkashi from Dehradun.
